Why this exists

EASA's regulatory framework is thorough, well-structured and publicly available — but navigating thousands of pages of PDF and the online EAR is time-consuming. Most people working in EU aviation need to find a specific rule, understand how it fits into the bigger picture, or quickly check what's changing.

This site translates the regulatory structure into an accessible, searchable reference that links directly to the authoritative source. It's built and maintained by an aviation professional, not a publisher.

This is an educational reference tool only. Always consult the official EASA Easy Access Rules for compliance purposes. See the disclaimer on each tool page.
